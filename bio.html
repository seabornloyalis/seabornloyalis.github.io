About Chad Myles

Naperville, Illinois. 1996-2013

Although born in New Jersey, Chad spent his childhood years in Naperville, Illinois, a suburb of Chicago. His younger sister was born there in 1997. Growing up, Chad struggled with reading before eventually overcoming that and developing a passion for fantasy fiction and classic literature. At Neuqua Valley High School, he met many close friends and took almost every class in the social sciences department offered.

Medford, Massachusetts. 2013-2017

Accepted into Tufts University, Chad initially intended to major in International Relations as a continuation of his focus on the social sciences. Taking his first computer science course, however, he quickly realized that he truly enjoyed software development and problem solving using computer science concepts. Eventually he determined to study Computer Science and minor in Film Studies to sate his love of movies.
